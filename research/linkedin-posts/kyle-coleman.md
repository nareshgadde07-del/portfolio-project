# Post 1 – Building an AI Habit Across the Marketing Team

**Topic:** AI Adoption & Change Management

### Summary

Kyle shares how he encouraged his marketing team to adopt AI by requiring every team member to submit one new AI use case each week. By actively engaging with every submission, he helped create lasting habits that dramatically increased AI usage and productivity across the organization.

### Main Takeaways

- AI adoption requires consistent leadership support.
- Small weekly experiments create long-term behavioral change.
- Technology alone doesn't transform organizations—culture does.

# Post 2 – The Five-Phase AI Transformation Playbook

**Topic:** AI Strategy

### Summary

Kyle explains the structured five-phase framework ClickUp used to scale AI across the company. The process included weekly experimentation, AI-powered workflow creation, centralized documentation, AI-driven workflow evaluation, and long-term coaching and hiring strategies.

### Main Takeaways

- Successful AI adoption follows a structured process.
- Documenting workflows helps organizations scale knowledge.
- AI can evaluate and improve existing AI systems.
