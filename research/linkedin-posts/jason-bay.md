# Post 2 – Enterprise Sales Lessons from Motive's Former CRO

**Topic:** Enterprise Sales Strategy

### Summary

Jason summarized key insights from an interview with Adam Block, former CRO of Motive. The discussion focused on improving enterprise sales by reducing account sizes, simplifying account ownership, and ensuring every sales representative can clearly explain the company's value proposition.

### Main Takeaways

- Smaller account books improve salesperson productivity.
- Assign one clear owner for each customer account.
- Every sales representative should clearly communicate the company's value within 90 seconds.

# Post 3 – Why Cold Calling Still Matters

**Topic:** Outbound Prospecting

### Summary

Jason argues that many AI-powered outbound automation strategies resemble traditional mass email campaigns. He believes personalized cold calling and face-to-face outreach remain highly effective because they face much less competition than automated email outreach.

### Main Takeaways

- Avoid mass automated outreach.
- Personal conversations create stronger engagement.
- Cold calling remains an effective outbound channel.

# Post 5 – The Best Cold Call Opener

**Topic:** Cold Calling Best Practices

### Summary

Based on research across more than 250 sales teams and hundreds of thousands of cold calls, Jason explains that successful cold call openers share three core elements: a greeting, one or more of Trigger, Tension, or Trust, and a permission-based question.

### Main Takeaways

- Begin with a clear introduction.
- Use triggers, customer pain points, or social proof.
- Ask permission before beginning the conversation.
- Personalization consistently outperforms generic scripts.
