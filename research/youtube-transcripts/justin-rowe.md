# Justin Rowe – Impactable Origin Story & Vision

## Executive Summary

Justin Rowe shares the journey of building Impactable from a side hustle into one of the world's largest LinkedIn Ads agencies. He explains the company's evolution, strategic pivots, culture, long-term vision, and why every new employee plays a role in building a category-defining business.

## Key Takeaways

- Justin transitioned from restaurant management into entrepreneurship by leveraging LinkedIn for networking and lead generation.
- His initial LinkedIn outreach strategy evolved into a freelance business, eventually becoming the agency Link N Learn.
- The agency rapidly scaled from a handful of clients to over 200 active accounts, serving more than 1,000 companies across 30+ countries.
- Rapid growth exposed operational bottlenecks, teaching the importance of scaling systems before scaling revenue.
- Recognizing LinkedIn's crackdown on automated outreach, the company proactively pivoted toward LinkedIn Ads before the market shifted.
- The agency was acquired by an experienced technology entrepreneur who invested in the business while keeping the leadership team intact.
- Transitioning to LinkedIn Ads created a more stable, higher-value, and longer-lasting business model.
- Strategic investment enabled the company to build proprietary tools, automation systems, custom CRM capabilities, and advanced data infrastructure.
- Impactable intentionally hires people with backgrounds in sales, hospitality, retail, telecom, and customer service rather than traditional marketing.
- The company's competitive advantage comes from specialization rather than trying to be a full-service marketing agency.
- Impactable believes it manages more LinkedIn Ads accounts than any other agency, positioning itself as a leader in a niche market.
- The agency works with funded startups, billion-dollar technology companies, and high-profile organizations despite maintaining a lean startup culture.
- Long-term success depends on continuous innovation, adaptability, and disciplined execution rather than chasing rapid growth.
- The vision is to become the world's leading LinkedIn Ads agency while reaching a company valuation of $50–100 million over the next five to ten years.

---



## Actionable Insights

- Look for underserved niches where specialization creates a strong competitive advantage.
- Build expertise in one channel before expanding into additional services.
- Treat market changes as opportunities to pivot instead of waiting until disruption forces change.
- Prioritize sustainable growth by strengthening systems, operations, and processes before aggressively scaling.
- Invest in automation, proprietary technology, and internal tools that improve efficiency and client outcomes.
- Hire for curiosity, adaptability, work ethic, and problem-solving—not just industry experience.
- Position your company around a clear, differentiated identity instead of competing as a generalist.
- Develop thought leadership to attract larger clients and strengthen brand authority.
- Focus on serving high-value clients through deep specialization rather than offering every possible marketing service.
- Set ambitious long-term goals while building the operational foundation required to achieve them.
